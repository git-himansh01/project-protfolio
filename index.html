About Me

A science student fascinated with art.

Hi, I’m Yash Kiran, a former software developer turned cinematographer and freelance photographer / content creator. After spending 3 years in the tech industry, I followed my passion for visual storytelling and transitioned into the world of photography and videography. I've been honing my skills since college, capturing moments with a creative eye and a deep love for the art. As a content creator, I specialize in travel and street photography, constantly inspired by the world around me. Whether it’s exploring new places or capturing everyday life, I aim to create authentic, compelling content that resonates with audiences. Let’s bring your vision to life through stunning visuals!

We are a team of photographers and filmmakers who are passionate about our work and we love to create content that is visually appealing and emotionally engaging with the target audience.
